love is

love takes many forms.
here are the 'loves' that I have learned from us.

check back in every day for the next 17 days for the next item!







17. the young love. the rush of air as you catch a glimpse of their eyes on yours. oh I always wished I could just pause that moment, to look into your eyes deeply and say nothing at all. the excitment of them wanting to even be around you. the admittance that you wanted to be around them too - that you wished for them to be around all the time. the realization that you were ready, so ready to let this person into your life. this love is precious and effervescent.

the young love is a multitude of firsts. that first kiss, that first stargaze, still fill me with butterflies because of the novelty of sharing these new things with you. I felt myself sparkling when I was around you. the world was bright, and it gave birth to the beginning of a greater love. love is bright.


16. the shoulder love. it's been a hard day. all you want and all you need, is your lovu by your side. this is the love on those days where hope feels faint and the weight of the world seems to have fallen on your shoulders. but we always have each other to lift some of that off. a shoulder you can come to and rest your head on, and know it will all be okay. and regardless of where either of us are, whether physically next to each other or across the world, that shoulder is always there.

so whether it's a good day or a bad day, love will always be there to hold you. love is supportive.
