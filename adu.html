17. the young love. the rush of air as you catch a glimpse of their eyes on yours. oh I always wished I could just pause that moment, to look into your eyes deeply and say nothing at all. the excitment of them wanting to even be around you. the admittance that you wanted to be around them too - that you wished for them to be around all the time. the realization that you were ready, so ready to let this person into your life. this love is precious and effervescent.

the young love is a multitude of firsts. that first kiss, that first stargaze, still fill me with butterflies because of the novelty of sharing these new things with you. I felt myself sparkling when I was around you. the world was bright, and it gave birth to the beginning of a greater love. love is bright.


16. the shoulder love. it's been a hard day. all you want and all you need, is your lovu by your side. this is the love on those days where hope feels faint and the weight of the world seems to have fallen on your shoulders. but we always have each other to lift some of that off. a shoulder you can come to and rest your head on, and know it will all be okay. and regardless of where either of us are, whether physically next to each other or across the world, that shoulder is always there.

so whether it's a good day or a bad day, love will always be there to hold you. love is supportive.


15. the tough love. yes, the love that you hate. this is the love that each of us needs to hear once in a while, and it is a testament to the strength of our relationship that we can really speak to each other truthfully. it's never what we want to hear, but it's always what we need. this is the stuff that makes us stronger and better people, and I'm so fortunate that I have my best friend to always keep me in check. it always makes me less afraid to face the rest of the world when I have you. love is hard.


14. the long distance love. it's not always easy. in fact, more often that not, it makes you feel empty inside. but the degree of that pain, that emptiness, is a testament to the degree of joy, of fullness that our love together brings. you were right in saying that it takes a special kind of relationship to be able to handle so much time apart, but that's us. even just two months into our relationship, we knew this was something that had to be sustained at all costs. summers have not been easy, but they have always been worth it. love is commitment.

9. the strong love. you know the best thing i’ve learned from this relationship? the concept of “no matter what”. that no matter how stupid someone’s being, you’ll have someone there. no matter where you are, you’ll try to make them feel comforted and supported. no matter how difficult things get, we’ll work through. everything, no matter what. and well, that’s not a common occurrence. because people fight and break apart over many things, but the test of a relationship, of the love between two people, is withstanding that strain. it’s okay to be stupid, to be wrong, to make mistakes, because we will love each other, no matter what. all the potential problems seem so damn trivial when i think about the pure happiness you bring to me. so i will love you, no matter what. love is resilient.
